Welcome to the Homepage of Tanbir Ahmed, Ph. D.

About

Hello! Thank you for visiting my homepage. I am an experimental mathematician with specialization in Extremal combinatorics and Combinatorial Optimization. I received my M. Sc. in Computer Science degree (2009) under the supervision of Prof. Vašek Chvátal in the area of combinatorial optimization and my Ph. D. in Computer Science degree (2013) under the supervision of Prof. Clement Lam in the area of extremal combinatorics, both from Concordia University.
Research interests:
Computer science:
SAT, Combinatorial Search, Data Structures and Algorithms, High Performance Computing
Math:
Integer & Zero-Sum Ramsey Theory, Discrete Geometry, Algebra, Graphs
